How the beta channel works: every change merged to main is built, tested, and published automatically. The beta app checks for updates shortly after launch and every few hours, downloads them in the background, and installs on restart (or the next quit). To leave the beta, uninstall it and install the stable build. Gaze features need the free Beam Eye Tracker; timer cues work without it.
