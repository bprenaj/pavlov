Feedback

You tunnel. Everyone does.

MapSense pings you every time you forget the minimap, until checking it is a reflex you don't think about.

Mark Your Minimap

Draw a box where your minimap sits, then press Enter. Presets for League of Legends, Dota 2, CS2 and more live in Settings.

Pick Your Bell

AudioA ding that stops the moment you check the map.
VisualYour minimap flashes until you look at it.
IRLWebhooks fire a real buzzer or LED you wire up.
SilentNo alerts. MapSense just keeps score.

Combine any of them in Settings.

Timers guess. Your eyes don't.

Free mode runs on a timer plus an "I checked the map" button. Pro mode reads your real glances through Beam Eye Tracker, a webcam eye tracker, so alerts and stats follow what your eyes actually did. Pro is paid; the first 7 days are free, no card.

Know Your Number

Every session ends in a Map Awareness Score, 0 to 100. It blends how often you check (pros glance 6-8 times a minute), how fast you get back, glance speed, and consistency. History charts all of it over time.
